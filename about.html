MI(SUL)GWAN

Tiara Kiatama's Art & Design Archive

Started in 2020, MI(SUL)GWAN is an archive of Tiara Kiatama's Art & Design Portfolio through the years. Infuenced by the Korean Culture and Tiara's Chinese heritage, the name MI(SUL)GWAN is a combination of the Korean derivation of Tiara's Chinese name (Mei Gwan, Mi Gwan) and the Korean word for Art Gallery (미술관, MiSulGwan).

EARLY EDUCATION (2007 - 2019)

Prior to High School, Tiara finishes her education at Anderson School, Nusaloka, BSD. She spent her Kindergarten, Elementary, and Middle School in the same school. Despite the stellar academic records, she has a higher interest in art, thus joining several art extracurricular activities. However, she had yet to start her Art and Design Portfolio then, which she will start as soon as she enter the highschool.

HIGH SCHOOL (2019 - 2022)

Fortunately, Tiara was able to join the Creative and Design elective during her high school years in UPH College. The opportunity push her to learn and practice art and design prior to university. Tiara create her portfolio since then. By now, her portfolio include some paintings, digital illustration, UX/UI and some great skills in handling professional design application such as Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.

VISUAL COMMUNICATION DESIGN (2022)

With an extra experience of internship at the end of her High School year, Tiara enter Pelita Harapan University's Visual Communication Design and start her journey further into the Creative Art and Design industry. The whole year includes the basic principles of Art and Design such as drawing, composing, photography, and more.

GRAPHIC DESIGN (2023 - )

On Tiara's second year in the university, the Visual Communication and Design is divided further into a further specialization. Tiara took a specialization in Graphic Design and thus delving into projects such as Book and Brand Identity Design.
